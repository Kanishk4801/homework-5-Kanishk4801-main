The Wild Spectrum: Species Varieties in U.S. National Parks

This visualization artfully merges a stacked bar chart with an integrated treemap to delve into the rich biodiversity found across U.S. national parks, each categorized by state. Each bar in the chart symbolizes a different national park, positioned along the y-axis by state. Nested within these bars, the treemap segments further detail the data, with each section highlighting a specific species category and its prevalence within that park. This thoughtful design enables the museum exhibit viewers to effortlessly gain insights into both the broad biodiversity of the parks and the intricate makeup of species that each park hosts. Marks:
The primary marks used are the lines (rectangular bars) of the stacked bar chart and the smaller rectangles (cells) of the treemap within each bar.

Channels:
• Position: The vertical position of each bar correlates to the state, providing geographical context.
• Length: The length of each bar indicates the total species count in each national park, allowing for comparisons across parks.
• Color: Colors in the treemap cells differentiate species categories, aiding in distinguishing between them visually.
• Area: The area of each treemap cell reflects the count of the species within that category, giving a sense of proportion at a glance.

Interactions:
• Tooltip: Hovering over any cell in the treemap displays a tooltip with detailed information about the park, state, species category, and count, enriching the data exploration experience with on-demand details.
• Legend: A color-coded legend aids in interpreting the colors used within the treemap, enhancing understandability.

This "Custom Visualization" is categorized as Combinatorial because it blends features from both stacked bar charts and treemaps into a unified display. Instead of simply showcasing a traditional stacked bar chart or an isolated treemap, it also incorporates a treemap within each segment of the bar chart. This integrated approach enhances the depth and richness of the data presentation, offering a more dynamic and informative visual experience.
